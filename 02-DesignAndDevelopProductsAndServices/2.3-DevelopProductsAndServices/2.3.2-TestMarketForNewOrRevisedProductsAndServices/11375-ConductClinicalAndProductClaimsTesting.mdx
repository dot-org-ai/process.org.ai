# Conduct clinical and product claims testing

> Business-as-Code definition for conduct clinical and product claims testing. Models testing the market for new or revised products and services through consumer testing, regulatory review, and commercial validation.

## Overview

Designing and executing clinical trials and product claims testing to validate product efficacy, safety, and marketing claims before market launch. Establishing test protocols, recruiting appropriate test populations, and conducting controlled studies that generate statistically significant evidence to support product benefit claims. Documenting test results, obtaining necessary certifications, and ensuring that all product claims meet regulatory requirements and can withstand legal and scientific scrutiny.
