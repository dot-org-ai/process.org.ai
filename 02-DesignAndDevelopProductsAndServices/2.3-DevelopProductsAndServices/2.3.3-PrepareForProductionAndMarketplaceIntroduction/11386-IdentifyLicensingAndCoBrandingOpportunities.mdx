# Identify licensing and co-branding opportunities

> Business-as-Code definition for identify licensing and co-branding opportunities. Models preparing for production and marketplace introduction, including material procurement, process validation, and channel coordination.

## Overview

Evaluating potential licensing agreements, co-branding partnerships, and strategic alliances to extend market reach and revenue potential for new products. Assessing partner brand alignment, market complementarity, and financial terms. Negotiating licensing fee structures, co-branding guidelines, and intellectual property usage rights. Identifying opportunities to leverage established brand equity through strategic partnerships that accelerate market penetration and enhance product credibility.
