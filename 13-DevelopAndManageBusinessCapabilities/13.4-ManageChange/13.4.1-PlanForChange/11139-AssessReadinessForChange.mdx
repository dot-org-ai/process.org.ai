# Assess readiness for change

> Business-as-Code definition for assess readiness for change. Models the process of determining the level of preparedness of the conditions, attitudes, and resources at all levels in the organization needed for change to happen successfully.

## Overview

Determining the level of preparedness of the conditions, attitudes, and resources at all levels in the organization needed for change to happen successfully. Define scope of the proposed change. Select the tools for a change readiness assessment.
