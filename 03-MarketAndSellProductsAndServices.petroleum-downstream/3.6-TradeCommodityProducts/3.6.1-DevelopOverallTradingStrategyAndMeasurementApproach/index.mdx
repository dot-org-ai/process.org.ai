# Develop overall trading strategy and measurement approach

> TODO: Business-as-Code definition for develop overall trading strategy and measurement approach (petroleum-downstream)

## Overview

Defining key trading objectives and evaluation criteria for commodity trading.  The long term market dynamics are assessed and analyzed and based on this, possible strategies to allow the organization to achieve the defined objectives are developed. Each of the strategies is then tested under various market and environmental scenarios which enables a determination of the trade-offs associated with each and the selection and refinement of the strategy that is the best fit.
