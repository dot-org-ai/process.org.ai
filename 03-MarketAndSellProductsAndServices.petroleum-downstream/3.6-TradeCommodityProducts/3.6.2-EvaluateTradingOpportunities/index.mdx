> TODO: Business-as-Code definition for evaluate trading opportunities (petroleum-downstream)

## Overview

Gathering and analyzing market data using various quantitative and qualitative techniques. Discover potential value in opportunities consistent with the trading strategy and objectives defined in Develop overall trading strategy and measurement approach [19520] and within established capital allocation and risk limits. An example is offering opportunities for discount acquisition of distressed crude or additives.  The environment is constantly monitored for trading opportunities availability, price, and compatibility of distressed crude.
