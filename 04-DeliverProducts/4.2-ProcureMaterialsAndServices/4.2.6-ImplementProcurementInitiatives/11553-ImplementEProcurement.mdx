# Implement e-procurement

> Business-as-Code definition for implement e-procurement. Models the procurement-initiatives processes within procurement to ensure materials and services are acquired efficiently and cost-effectively.

## Overview

Deploying electronic procurement platforms and tools to automate and streamline purchasing workflows. E-procurement systems digitize requisitions, approvals, purchase orders, and invoice processing to reduce cycle times, improve compliance, and provide spend visibility.
