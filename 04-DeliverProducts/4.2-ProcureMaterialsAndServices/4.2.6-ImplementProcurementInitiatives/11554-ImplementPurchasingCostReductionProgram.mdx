# Implement purchasing cost reduction program

> Business-as-Code definition for implement purchasing cost reduction program. Models the procurement-initiatives processes within procurement to ensure materials and services are acquired efficiently and cost-effectively.

## Overview

Implementing systematic programs to reduce the total cost of purchased materials and services. Cost reduction programs employ techniques such as value engineering, supplier consolidation, contract renegotiation, and demand management to achieve sustainable savings.
