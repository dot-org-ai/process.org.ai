# Implement buying consortiums

> Business-as-Code definition for implement buying consortiums. Models the procurement-initiatives processes within procurement to ensure materials and services are acquired efficiently and cost-effectively.

## Overview

Creating formal arrangements with other organizations to pool purchasing volumes and leverage combined buying power. Buying consortiums enable smaller organizations to access volume-based pricing, improved service levels, and supplier terms that would otherwise be unavailable to individual buyers.
