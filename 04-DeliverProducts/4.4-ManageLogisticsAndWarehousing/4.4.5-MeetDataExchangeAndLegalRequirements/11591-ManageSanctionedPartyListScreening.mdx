# Manage sanctioned party list screening

> Business-as-Code definition for manage sanctioned party list screening. Models the trade compliance processes to ensure efficient movement, storage, and compliance of goods throughout the supply chain.

## Overview

Screening trading partners, customers, and suppliers against government-maintained sanctioned party lists. Verify that transactions do not involve denied, blocked, or restricted entities as required by trade compliance regulations.
