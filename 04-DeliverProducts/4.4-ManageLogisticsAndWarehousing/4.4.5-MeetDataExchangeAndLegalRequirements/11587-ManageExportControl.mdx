# Manage export control

> Business-as-Code definition for manage export control. Models the trade compliance processes to ensure efficient movement, storage, and compliance of goods throughout the supply chain.

## Overview

Administering export control regulations to ensure compliance with national security and foreign policy requirements. Screen transactions against restricted party lists, validate end-use certificates, and obtain required export licenses.
