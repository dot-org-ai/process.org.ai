# Calculate customs duty

> Business-as-Code definition for calculate customs duty. Models the trade compliance processes to ensure efficient movement, storage, and compliance of goods throughout the supply chain.

## Overview

Determining the customs duties and taxes applicable to imported and exported goods. Apply tariff schedules, trade agreement preferences, and valuation rules to calculate the correct duty amounts for international shipments.
