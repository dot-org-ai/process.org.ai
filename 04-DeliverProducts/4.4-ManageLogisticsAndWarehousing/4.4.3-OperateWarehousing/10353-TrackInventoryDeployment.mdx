# Track inventory deployment

> Business-as-Code definition for track inventory deployment. Models the warehousing processes to ensure efficient movement, storage, and compliance of goods throughout the supply chain.

## Overview

Tracking the logistical act of delivering or releasing an inventory item or entity to targeted end users. Track how much inventory has been deployed at all the distribution centers, individually.
