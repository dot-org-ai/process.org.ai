# Monitor inbound delivery performance

> Business-as-Code definition for monitor inbound delivery performance. Models the inbound logistics processes to ensure efficient movement, storage, and compliance of goods throughout the supply chain.

## Overview

Overseeing the performance of an inbound delivery system. Check the present delivery system's efficiency, cost effectiveness, and adherence to a delivery schedule.
